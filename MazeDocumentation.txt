The program begins with a default 10x10 unbiased random maze.

To begin depth first search:
press "d"
* the search will end once the exit has been reached

To begin breadth first search:
press "b"
* the search will end once the exit has been reached

To toggle the visibility of visited paths:
press " "

To move around:
press right, left, up, and down
* can only move if search is not occurring
* your current location is the yellow square
* once you've reached the exit, you win!

To produce a new unbiased maze of random size:
press "n"

To produce a random vertically biased maze:
press "v"

To produce a random horizontally biased maze:
press "h"
